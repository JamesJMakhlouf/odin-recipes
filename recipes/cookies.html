Tasty Chocolate Chip Cookies

This chocolate chip cookie recipe makes dozens of delicious cookies with crisp edges and chewy middles loaded with chocolate chips!

Ingredients

  • Butter - 1 cup
  • Brown Sugar - 1 cup
  • White Sugar - 1/2 cup
  • 2 Eggs
  • Vanilla - 2 teaspoons
  • Flour - 2 1/2 cups
  • Baking Soda - 1 teaspoon
  • Salt - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Chocolate Chips - 1 1/2 cups

Steps

  1. Cream butter and sugars. Add eggs and vanilla.
  2. Mix dry ingredients separately, then combine. Fold in chocolate chips.
  3. Scoop onto lined baking sheet. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 10-12 min.
