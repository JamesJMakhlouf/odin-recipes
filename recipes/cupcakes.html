Delicious Cupcakes

I made these white chocolate raspberry cupcakes for my step-daughter's engagement party and they were a huge hit!

Ingredients

For the Batter:

  • Milk - 1/2 cup
  • Butter - 1/2 cup
  • Vanilla - 1 teaspoom
  • Sugar - 1 cup
  • 2 Eggs
  • Flour - 1 1/2 cups

For the Frosting:

  • Butter - 1/2 cup
  • Powdered Sugar - 2 cups
  • Vanilla - 1 teaspoon
  • Milk - 2 tablespoons

Steps

  1. Cream butter and sugar, add eggs and vanilla.
  2. Mix dry ingredients separately, then combine, alternating with milk.
  3. Fill cupcake liners 2/3 full, bake at 180°C (350°F) for 18-22 min.
  4. Beat frosting ingredients, spread or pipe onto cooled cupcakes.
